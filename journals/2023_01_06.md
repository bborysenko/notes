- ## [[How to quickly create a new Quick Note on macOS?]]
	- To [start a Quick Note on your Mac](https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/notes/apdf028f7034/mac) just press and hold the `Fn` key and then press `Q`. If you want always start a new Quick Note (instead of opening the previous one), choose Notes > Settings, and then deselect “Always resume to last Quick Note”.
